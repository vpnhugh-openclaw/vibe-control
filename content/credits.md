# Credits & Models

Treat credits like runway.

## What belongs here

- Monthly, daily, and promo credits
- Whether a promo is confirmed, fresh, or expired
- Which models are worth using for which tasks
- Quick guidance on where to spend paid usage vs where to stay free

## Priority rules

1. Use free or daily-reset credits for exploration.
2. Save paid, high-quality models for rescue work, architecture, or launch-blocking tasks.
3. Spend expiring promo credits before monthly renewable credits.

## Weekly review

- What credits are about to expire?
- Which platforms have fresh free-tier value right now?
- Which model is best for coding vs research vs UI generation?
