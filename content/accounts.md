# Accounts

Keep each account intentional.

## What belongs here

- Which login owns which builds
- What each account is best used for
- Last-used recency
- Platform fit and overlap
- Any obvious hygiene issues, like stale accounts or unclear ownership

## Decision rules

1. Use **Primary** for main shipping work and anything tied to core infrastructure.
2. Use **Pharmacy** for client-facing pharmacy builds and experiments you may want separated from personal work.
3. Use **Alt Dev** for spare credits, fresh free tiers, and isolated tests.

## Review checklist

- Is each account still active?
- Does every account have a clear purpose?
- Are credit-limited platforms paired with the right account?
- Is there any account that should be retired, merged, or renamed?
